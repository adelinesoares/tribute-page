Ayrton Senna da Silva

One of the great brasilians idols

Here's a time line of Ayrton Senna life:

  • 1981 - Senna began his motorsport career in karting;
  • 1983 - He won the British Formula Three Championship;
  • 1985 - He won six Grands Prix over the next three seasons;
  • 1988 - he joined Frenchman Alain Prost at McLaren. Between them, they won all but one of the 16 Grands Prix that year, and Senna claimed his first World Championship;
